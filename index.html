A tour of NUV

After finally setting foot on NUV's campus, you're excited to explore it. However, the new student advisors have hidden six clues in the format nuvctf{number_text} all across NUV. To complete the scavenger hunt, you must merge all the parts into one in order. For example, if you find the clues nuvctf{1_lOsT}, nuvctf{2__!N_b} (note the repeated underscore), and nuvctf{3_03LT3r}, the answer is nuvctf{lOsT_!N_b03LT3r}. Have fun exploring!
